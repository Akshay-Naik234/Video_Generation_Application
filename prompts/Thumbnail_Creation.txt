Generate a cinematic, ultra-sharp YouTube documentary thumbnail in 16:9 (1280x720, RGB).

SIMPLICITY IS EVERYTHING (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The thumbnail MUST pass the "1-inch test" — it must be instantly readable at mobile phone size (1 inch wide).
Maximum THREE visual elements total: (1) the face, (2) ONE subtle background element, (3) text overlay.
If a viewer has to study the thumbnail to understand it, you have FAILED.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
FACE — THE DOMINANT ELEMENT (70% OF FRAME)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Create a chest-up close-up portrait of {{PERSON_NAME}} at the age that represents their most iconic or dramatic moment.

Composition:
- Face fills 70% of the frame — this is the #1 factor for click-through rate
- Use a subtle 3/4 angle with rule-of-thirds positioning
- Face fully visible, eyes unobstructed and making direct eye contact with the viewer
- Leave clean negative space on one side (left or right) for the text overlay

Historical accuracy:
- Authentic period-accurate clothing, grooming, and posture — no modern styling
- Documentary realism only

Facial expression:
- ONE dominant emotion that matches the story's core tension (intense stare, quiet defiance, controlled fury, haunted regret, cold calculation)
- Convey emotion through subtle micro-expressions: eyebrow tension, jaw set, eye intensity
- Natural skin texture with realistic imperfections — visible pores, subtle asymmetry, natural under-eye shadows
- NOT smooth, NOT airbrushed, NOT AI-looking

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
LIGHTING — CINEMATIC DEPTH
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Dramatic split lighting (Rembrandt or strong side light):
- One side of the face lit in warm golden/amber tones
- The opposite side in cool blue/teal shadows
- Strong HDR clarity with professional color grading
- Subtle film grain for cinematic texture
- The lighting itself should tell a story (tension, duality, mystery)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
BACKGROUND — MINIMAL AND NON-DISTRACTING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Dark, deeply blurred background with ONLY ONE faint semi-transparent symbolic element related to {{PERSON_NAME}}'s story:
- Keep background elements at 15-20% opacity maximum
- Examples: faint flames, scattered money, a single equation, a courtroom outline, a flag silhouette
- Background MUST NOT compete with the face for attention
- NO multiple props, NO badges, NO seals, NO charts, NO documents, NO text in the background
- Add subtle atmospheric depth: soft light rays OR floating dust particles (not both)

TEXT OVERLAY (ADDED IN POST-PRODUCTION)

DO NOT embed text in the image prompt — text is added separately.

The overlay_text field should contain exactly 2-4 BOLD UPPERCASE WORDS that:
- Create instant emotional impact or curiosity
- Work standalone without the title (a viewer seeing ONLY these words should feel compelled to click)
- Are derived from the single most dramatic moment in the story

Text style (applied in post-production):
- Bold sans-serif (Bebas Neue / Montserrat Black / Impact style)
- High contrast white or warm gold with subtle drop shadow
- Placed in the clean negative space — NEVER covering the face
- Large enough to read at mobile phone size
